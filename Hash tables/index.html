What You Need
to Know

We believe in complete transparency. If your question isn't answered here, our attorneys are ready to speak with you directly — at no obligation.


Speak to an Attorney →

We focus exclusively on high-stakes commercial, corporate, and white-collar matters. Our practice areas include corporate litigation, mergers & acquisitions, securities law, white-collar criminal defense, international arbitration, and executive employment. We do not handle personal injury, family law, or routine transactional work.

Our fees are structured on an hourly basis for most matters, with rates that reflect the seniority and specialization of the attorneys involved. For certain commercial litigation matters, we are open to discussing alternative fee arrangements, including contingency or hybrid structures, based on the nature of the case. All fee arrangements are clearly documented before any engagement begins.

For urgent matters, we offer same-day consultations. For standard inquiries, we typically respond within one business day to schedule your initial consultation. During the consultation, a senior attorney—not a paralegal or associate—will assess your matter and provide an honest evaluation of your position and options.

Yes. While our primary offices are in New York City, we practice regularly in federal courts across the country and handle international arbitration matters globally. For matters in other jurisdictions, we work seamlessly with local counsel where required to ensure full compliance with jurisdictional rules.

Bring any relevant documents, correspondence, contracts, or prior court filings related to your matter. The more context you can provide, the more useful the initial consultation will be. If you are under any time-sensitive deadlines—such as a statute of limitations or court-ordered date—please communicate that immediately when scheduling so we can prioritize appropriately.

All consultations are fully protected by attorney-client privilege from the moment you engage with us. Nothing you share will ever be disclosed without your explicit consent. We take our ethical obligations to client confidentiality with the utmost seriousness and have maintained a perfect record in this regard throughout our history.
